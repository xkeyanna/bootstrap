About Me

After receiving a BA from UC Irvine in International Finance, I decided to travel the world. I started by teaching English in Hong Kong and then got hired by American Airlines to work as a flight attendant back in the USA. I began my flight attendant job in New York, went on to Miami, San Francisco, until finally I made it to my hometown, Orange County (now I work at LAX and SNA).

With a toddler at home, everything is different and I want to gain skills that I can put to work for different businesses of all kinds, without leaving the state, or the country. I have a passion for numbers, logic, and development and I'm overjoyed to be given the change to be a part of your team! Feel free to contact me if you have any questions or comments, I'd love to hear from you!
